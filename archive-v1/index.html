Night hiking and outdoor adventures

Our philosophy

Jobs fill your pocket, adventures fill your soul! Movement pioneers the art of night hiking, a new type of adventure for a new type of explorer! We organise regular outdoor activities, collect experiences and train together for international expeditions, kayaking trips and mountain climbing challenges.

We believe that although in night time your sight is limited, it makes you focus more on yourself and people around you. The real voyage of discovery consists not in seeking new landscapes but in having new eyes.
